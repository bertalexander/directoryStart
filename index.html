Best Boutiques in St. Louis

For the Trendy St. Louis Shopper

When I came to St. Louis, I didn’t realize the bubbling status of our local fashion community. As a part of the style department at St. Louis Magazine, I come across many amazing local and national boutiques we have here in St. Louis. If you see any of the shoots in the magazine, we have most likely used these local suppliers for clothing and accessories.

We have complied this directory of local boutiques to keep your wardrobe trendy and exciting. With every listing, we have provided the name of the boutique and accompanying website. For your convenience, we have also linked a Google maps listing for every address for easy navigation. Finally, since every boutique offers different assortment, we have listed some products that they sell.

While this list is extensive, it is not complete! If you have any recommendations to add to the directory or any general feedback, be sure to submit a message using the ‘Send Feedback!’ button below!
